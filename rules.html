  • Purpose of the Contest

    • This contest seeks to encourage StartUP Chile companies to know and experiment with 5G and encourage companies to design innovative value offers leveraged on this new technology. Through technological projects that significantly change people's lives or extend the frontier of possibilities to companies, which with current technology cannot be achieved.
    • Therefore, the entire StartUP Chile portfolio is invited to participate, telling us how 5G technology can add value to its businesses and customers through a video.
    • WOM will support 5 projects with business potential that aim to revolutionize their industry / market by incorporating 5G, showing the benefits that its implementation can generate and that serve as inspiration for the industry. To achieve this objective, it will provide permits, spaces to test, visibility and mentoring for the development of pilots on a real scale.
    • The solution can be implemented from any software (app, web, platform, etc.) or hardware development. The requirement is that its application with 5G technology (in its first phase of implementation) allows a completely differentiating solution from what exists today in the market and that generates the greatest possible impact.
    • Projects can have applications in various fields, such as education, health, gaming, retail, manufacturing and among many others.

  • Stages of the Contest

    • All Startups belonging to the StartUP Chile portfolio may be presented to the challenge.
    • The projects will be received from March 12th until April 8th, 2020 at 11:59 p.m. Applications received after this deadline will not be considered.
    • After a pre-selection process, on April 22nd will be published to the finalists, who will go to a demo day on May 15th where they will have the opportunity to present before a jury composed of experts in technology, business and innovation, chosen by WOM.
    • The 5 winners of the “WOM 5G Challenge” contest will be announced ending the day that same day.

  • Requirements, documents to present and selection process

    • a. Requirements to participate
    • The projects must come from companies in the StartUp Chile network (current or later generation). This information will be corroborated with StartUp Chile.
    • b. Documentation to be presented
    • To participate, the applicant companies must submit:
    • · Applicant identification form.
      · Copy of the Identity Card or Passport of the applicant.
      · Copy of the company's Tax Id.
      · Certificate of validity of the participating company.
      · Certificate of powers of the company, proving that the applicant has the quality of administrator.
      · Presentation video with a maximum duration of 3 minutes that presents the following:
      • Presentation of the company and team members.
      • Challenge to the one who postulates the idea.
      • Problem or pain of the client to solve.
      • Potential market size of the problem to be solved.
      • Why do you think 5G will improve life or solve customers' problem / pain.
      • How 5G will differentiate them and give added value to your proposal / solution.
      • Project exploration plan or phase.
      • Contact of any of the participants
    • c. Selection process
    • Stage 1: Pre selection of 15 contestants
    • After the closing of the call and verification of the fulfillment of the rules by the Applicants (the non-submission of documents or their filling out outside the rules of the contest, will imply the immediate elimination of the evaluation), a jury of experts will choose up to 15 Projects. This selection and definition will be final once the information is published. The official publication will be made through WOM´s website or official communication channels of the Startup Chile network on April 22nd.
    • Stage 2: Demo-day and selection of winners
    • Among the 15 shortlisted contestants, 5 winners will be chosen through a demo-day on May 15th. Each finalist will present their idea to a jury, in which they will have 15 minutes (10 of presentation and 5 of questions). Winners will be chosen based on the criteria defined in these rules.
    • d. Evaluation criteria
    • · Problem / Opportunity (25%): Size of the opportunity. The client, his needs and pains must be defined.
      · Proposal of the solution (25%): Degree of innovation. How are these problems to be solved using the potential of the 5G network. Demonstrate how 5G will boost your project against your competition.
      · Benefits (40%): Impact (social / economic) and business model (is the model viable, repeatable and scalable?).
      · Skills (10%): Do you have the skills to address the challenge and solution posed?

  • Awards

    • Four prizes will be awarded for each of the five winners of the Second Selection Stage.
    • The winners will have demonstrated that their solution to the proposed challenge is of great impact to people and / or industries is benefited by 5G widely compared to 4G.
    • For the winners, WOM establishes the following prizes for the 5 winners:
    • 1. The opportunity to take your pilot to real scale.
      2. WOM 5G labs tests.
      3. WOM style visibility.
      4. Mentoring by experts.
